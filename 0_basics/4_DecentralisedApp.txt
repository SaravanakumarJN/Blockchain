Decentralised App (Dapp)
  - User Interface + Smart contract

  Most common categories
  - Defi (Decentralsied finance)
  - Game
  - Gambling ., 


Architecture of Dapp
  - Smart contract layer (lives on blockchain and stores the sensitive information and business logic)
  - Frontend layer
  - Backend layer (optional)

Dapp development process
  - Data modelling
  - Development of smart contract
  - Test smart contract (Truffle)
  - Build frontend 
  - Deploy (first in testnet and then to mainnet)
